Welcome to Incycle

CREATING A ZERO WASTE SOCIETY

Our business is built around our customers and partners, serving them effectively in collection logistics, keeping all parties inter-connected for improved efficiency and convenience and ensuring getting “waste” back on tables.

We commit to continuous development and improvement of our processes and software with a goal of improving waste producer responsibility.

How do we achieve this?

Request for an instant pickup of their waste

This request is directed to a registered waste collection within the vicinity for the pick-up, and then directed to recycling companies on the platform that need specific grades and class of waste as raw materials.

Subscribe to platform for scheduled pickups

The mobile app comes with a scheduled calendar that is tailored to your location and tells you what day and time range our collectors will come around.

RECYCLING COMPANY

Connect and trade recyclable waste with an option of using our pickup logistics

Compensation

Incycle would directly compensate individuals who presort their waste per waste category per kilogram depending on our current compensation plan and market prices. Waste bills would paid through the mobile platforms through an integrated payment medium for convenience of our producers and ability to directly monitor how they are served from their payments.
